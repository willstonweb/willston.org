End of Summer Update: Thank you for your support! by Jonathan Buchholz on November 21, 2022

Here is a long overdue update on what happened after the bootcamp. Thank you so much to everyone who helped make this happen!

  • All twelve students completed the curriculum taught during the week-long bootcamp, got to keep their laptops, and went to Busch Gardens
  • Students were extremely engaged: one parent said, “they can’t stop talking about it at home…”
  • Five students completed freeCodeCamp's (New) Responsive Web Design Certificate (estimated 300 hours of work)
    • Three of them received a brand new MacBook Air
  • All students received cash prizes (at least $300)
  • One student helped another, who hadn’t participated in the bootcamp, complete Willston Web's curriculum
  • One student created a public-facing website

Don't worry—just updates.
