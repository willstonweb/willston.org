Learn web development at the Willston Center June 13-17, 2022!

We're teaching web development to high school students and giving away computers for students who have demonstrated a desire to learn.


Sign up! Donate

Details

Who?

We are looking for high school students who:

  • Live near the Willston Multicultural Center
  • Are interested in web development

What?

In partnership with Comunidad, Willston Web will host a bootcamp to teach web development. In the bootcamp, students will:

  • Understand how web servers, HTML, CSS, and DNS make up the fundamentals of the web
  • Learn HTML by building a cat photo app
  • Be ready to continue FreeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design course

When?

The Willston Web bootcamp will be held June 13-17, 2022 from 9:30am-1pm. Sustained mentorship for select students will continue throughout the 2022-2023 school year.

Where?

The Willston Web bootcamp will be held at the Willston Multicultural Center:

6131 Willston Drive Falls Church, VA, 22044

Why?

This program was created to solve a demonstrated need when Ricardo asked Jonathan to teach him how to do web development.

Learn more about our story!

How do I get a computer?

We have laptops for all students to use while in our program, but students will only be able to keep them if they meet all three of the following criteria:

  • Complete our curriculum
  • Do not own a computer
  • Show promise/potential in web development and programming
