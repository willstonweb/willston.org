First Day Complete: Students are interested and engaged! by Jonathan Buchholz on June 13, 2022

Today was the first day of the Willston Web Coding Bootcamp and it was a great success! Thank you to Ms. Maralee, Patrick, Rebecca, Ricardo, and my mom for showing up early to help set up. All 13 students we were expecting showed up and were very interested in learning. They stayed on task and were productive with their time. Many of them are eager to continue working and wanted to take their laptops home today so they could keep going through curriculum. The prospect of a premium laptop is quite the motivator! One student has limited English proficiency, but was still able to follow along with brief explanations in Spanish when needed.

Students learned how to use a new operating system (Pop!_OS), created HTML document structure out of paper, and completed steps 1-6 at a minimum of freeCodeCamp's (New) Responsive Web Design Certificate, where they learned about header and paragraph tags, comments, SEO (search engine optimization), and indentation.
